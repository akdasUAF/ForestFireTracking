Metric Information

p@50: Precision at 50% IoU threshold. Measures the accuracy of the positive predictions.

r@50: Recall at 50% IoU threshold. Measures the ability to find all relevant instances.

mAP@50: Mean Average Precision at 50% IoU threshold. Average precision across all classes.

mAP@50-95: Mean Average Precision across IoU thresholds from 50% to 95%. Provides a more comprehensive evaluation.
